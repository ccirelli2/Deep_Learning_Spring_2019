# CLASS NOTES:   RECCURENT NEURAL NETWORKS___________________________________________



Recurrent:  	Means repeat. Some things in a NN can be repeated. 
Application: 	Sequence modeling. 
		Can be applied to text, what word may come next in a sequency. 
		Can handle variable length sequences. 
		Track long term dependencies. 
		Can maintain information about order. 
		Share parameters across the sequence. 
		Sentiment Analysis, Music Generation.
